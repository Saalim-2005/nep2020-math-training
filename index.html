NEP-2020 Mathematics Teacher Training

Empowering Teaching for Understanding & Joyful Learning

📘 Training Overview

This training introduces key pedagogical reforms under the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020 for mathematics instruction...

  • 🧠 Competency-based and concept-focused learning
  • 🎨 Hands-on, joyful teaching strategies
  • 💻 Use of technology and arts in teaching
  • 👶 Age-appropriate goals for each stage
  • 📊 Assessment reforms and inclusive practices

🎯 Learning Objectives

  • Understand key pedagogical reforms...
  • Apply age-appropriate, activity-based strategies...
  • Integrate technology and arts...
  • Design competency-based lesson plans
  • Use assessment for learning...

👨‍🏫 About the Speaker

🧑‍🏫 Javaid Ahmad Bhat, also known as Javaid Jawad, is a passionate educator and the Headmaster of Government Middle School Onagam in Bandipora district, Jammu and Kashmir. With over two decades of experience, he has taught Mathematics ➕, Science 🔬, and English 📘, inspiring countless students through his engaging and value-driven teaching style. On September 5, 2021, he was honored with the Best Teacher Award 🏆 by the J&K Administration in recognition of his commitment, honesty, and tireless efforts in improving the quality of education. Under his leadership, student enrollment at his school doubled 📈, thanks to his innovative practices and strong community involvement. He is also an active volunteer with the J&K Yateem Foundation 🤝, where he works for educational upliftment and youth development. Respected widely as a role model 🌟, he continues to transform lives through education and service. 💬 Public Praise Described as an inspiring figure in rural education, especially for uplifting under-resourced schools and making them centers of active learning and discipline.
